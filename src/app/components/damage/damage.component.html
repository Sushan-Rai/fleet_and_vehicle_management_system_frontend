Guidelines

  • Select the exact vehicle registration number that incurred the damage.
  • The date defaults to today's date and cannot be in the future.
  • Provide a descriptive account of the damage so mechanics can prepare the right tools.
  • For Drivers, your driving score deduction (if any) will be audited and evaluated by the route's Fleet Manager.
